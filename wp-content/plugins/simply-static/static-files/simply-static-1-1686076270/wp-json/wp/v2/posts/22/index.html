Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina will officially inaugurate the construction of the country’s second subway from Hemayetpur to Bhatara. Officials of Dhaka Mass Transit Company Limited are talking about the possibility of officially opening the second subway construction work in July. No date has been set yet. However, MRT Line 5 Northern Route work is progressing ahead of the opening of the construction work in July. In the meantime, the land acquisition and the registration of the acquired land have been completed, said the officials concerned.<\/p>\n

The MRT Line 5 Northern Route project is underway to build a 20 km metro rail from Hemayetpur to Vatara by 2018. Out of the 20 km long route, 13.50 km of underground and 6.50 km of aerial infrastructure will be constructed. It will have 14 stations. Of these, nine are underground and five are airborne stations.<\/p>\n

Officials say that the feasibility study and basic design of the MRT Line 5 Northern Route have already been completed. Detailed design work is in progress. As of April, the detailed design of the project has progressed to 80 percent. After the financial evaluation of the tender for the Hemayetpur Depot land development package, the consent of the donor agency JICA has also been received. It is currently in the process of being presented to the board meeting of DMDCL for approval.<\/p>\n

About Tk 934.84 crore has been paid for the estimated amount of 99.25 acres land acquisition for construction of Hemayetpur Depot and Depot Access Corridor. The acquired land was taken over on October 3, 2022 and gazetted for 99.25 acres of land on October 20. In the meantime, land registration has been completed. The overall progress of land acquisition (Hemayetpur Depot) is 85% till the end of April.<\/p>\n

Dhaka Mass Transit Company Limited Managing Director MAM Chiddik told Bangla Tribune that the construction work of MRT Line 5 Northern Route is likely to be inaugurated by the Honorable Prime Minister in July. The construction of aeronautical and underground stations will be carried out efficiently by studying all aspects.<\/p>\n

Route Map of Northern Route<\/p>\n

From Hemayetpur Depot to Baliarpur, Bilamalia, Aminbazar, Gabtali, Darus Salam, Mirpur 1, Mirpur 10, Mirpur 14, Kachukshet, Banani, Gulshan 2, New Bazar, Bhatara. There will be flying stations from Hemayetpur to Aminbazar. Then Gabtali will go underground through various routes to the new market. And connect by flying to Vatara.<\/p>\n

Earlier, on December 28, 2022, Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina inaugurated the metro rail movement of MRT Line Six Metrorail from Uttara North to Agargaon.<\/p>\n

Besides, on February 2 this year, Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina inaugurated the construction of the first underground metro rail MRT line 1 airport route and Purbachal route.<\/p>\n
