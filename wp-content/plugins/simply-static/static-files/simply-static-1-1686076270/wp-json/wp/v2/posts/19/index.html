Christophe Gaultier could not keep his job after winning the French Ligue 1 title. French club Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) have cut him, ESPN reported.<\/p>\n

This season, PSG won Ligue 1 under him. But the club authorities decided to drop him as Champions League success was elusive. This time they had to leave after losing to Bayern Munich in the last sixteen.<\/p>\n

PSG sporting director Luis Campos broke the news of Gaultier’s sacking himself on Tuesday, ESPN reported. The club authorities are continuing to negotiate with Bayern coach Julian Nagelsmann to replace him.<\/p>\n

Citing sources, ESPN also reported that they have also spoken with former Spain and Barcelona coach Luis Enrique. But their main target is the Nagglesman. And the German coach wants Thierry Henry as his assistant.<\/p>\n

Galtier joined PSG last summer after leading Nice to ninth place in the 2021-22 season. Before that he also worked at Lille, Saint Etienne.<\/p>\n
